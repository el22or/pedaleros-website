## About This Tour

Experience the beauty of the Danube River on this half-day kayaking adventure. Our expert guides will lead you through calm waters, showcasing the natural beauty and wildlife of the region.

### What's Included

- Professional kayaking equipment (kayak, paddle, life jacket)
- Experienced local guide
- Safety briefing and paddling instruction
- Waterproof bag for personal belongings
- Light refreshments and water

### What to Bring

- Swimwear and change of clothes
- Sunscreen and sunglasses
- Water bottle
- Towel
- Camera (optional - bring waterproof case)

### Meeting Point

We meet at the Sombor Kayak Center, 15 minutes before departure. Free parking available on-site.

### Important Information

- Minimum age: 12 years
- Swimming ability required
- Maximum group size: 10 participants
- Tours run daily from April to October, weather permitting
